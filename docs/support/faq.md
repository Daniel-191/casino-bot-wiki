# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common Paradise Bot questions.

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I get started with Paradise Bot?
**A:** Use `/daily` to get starting money, then try simple games like `/coinflip 100 heads`. Check `/help` for more commands.

### Q: Why can't I use commands?
**A:** Make sure the bot has proper permissions and you're in an allowed channel. Contact your server admin if issues persist.

### Q: How do I get more money?
**A:** Use `/daily` and `/hourly` for free money. Play games, complete jobs, try farming, or participate in heists for more earnings.

## Economy Questions

### Q: Can I lose money I put in the bank?
**A:** No! Money in your bank account is completely safe and cannot be lost through gambling or other activities.

### Q: What's the daily limit for gambling?
**A:** This varies by server settings. Use `/limits` to check your current limits.

### Q: Can I trade items with other players?
**A:** Yes! Use `/trade @user` to initiate trades. Both players must confirm before the trade completes.

### Q: Do I earn interest on bank money?
**A:** This depends on your server's configuration. Some servers offer small daily interest on banked funds.

## Game Questions

### Q: Are the games fair?
**A:** Yes! All games use certified random number generation.

### Q: Why did I lose a coin flip?
**A:** Coin flips are 50/50 chance. Losing streaks happen naturally - that's just variance.

### Q: Can I play games against other players?
**A:** Absolutely! Try `/duel @user`, `/fight @user`, or join a `/heist` for multiplayer fun.

### Q: What's the maximum bet?
**A:** Maximum bets vary by game and server settings. Use `/help <game>` to see specific limits.

## Technical Issues

### Q: The bot isn't responding to my commands
**A:** Try these steps:
1. Make sure you're using slash commands (`/` prefix)
2. Check if the bot is online (green status)
3. Verify the bot has message permissions
4. Try in a different channel

### Q: My balance disappeared!
**A:** Balances don't disappear without reason. Check:
- Recent game history with `/history`
- If someone robbed you (check notifications)
- Server-wide economy events or resets

### Q: Commands are slow or timing out
**A:** This usually indicates high bot load or Discord API issues. Wait a few minutes and try again.

## Social Features

### Q: How do heists work?
**A:** Heists are group activities. Use `/heist join` when someone starts one. Success depends on team size and luck.

### Q: Can I give money to friends?
**A:** Yes! Use `/pay @user <amount>` to send money directly to other players.

### Q: How do I report inappropriate behavior?
**A:** Use `/report @user <reason>` or contact server moderators directly.

## Advanced Features

### Q: What are properties and how do they work?
**A:** Properties provide passive income. Buy them with `/property buy <name>` and earn daily rent payments.

### Q: How does farming work?
**A:** Plant crops with `/farm plant <crop>`, wait for them to grow, then harvest for profit. Different crops have different growth times and values.

### Q: Can I automate activities?
**A:** No, all commands must be run manually. Automation/botting is against the rules and can result in penalties.

## Rules & Safety

### Q: What happens if I break the rules?
**A:** Violations can result in temporary mutes, balance reductions, or permanent bans depending on severity.

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A:** Yes, we follow Discord's privacy policies and don't store unnecessary personal information.

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
**A:** No, one account per person is allowed. Multi-accounting results in all accounts being banned.
