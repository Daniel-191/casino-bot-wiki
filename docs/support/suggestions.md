# Suggestions

Have an idea for a new game, feature, or improvement? Use [`/suggest`](#suggest) - it goes straight to the dev's suggestion channel and creates a discussion thread.

## /suggest

```
/suggest suggestion:"<your idea>" [attachment]
```

### Required Fields

- **suggestion** - Your idea. The more specific, the better.

### Optional Fields

- **attachment** - Image to help explain. Image attachments only.

### Example

```
/suggest suggestion:"Add a /lottery-history command that shows my past tickets and whether they won"
```

## What happens after

1. Your suggestion is posted in the suggestions channel.
2. Two reactions are added automatically: 👍 and 👎 - community voting.
3. A **discussion thread** is auto-created (7-day archive timer) where staff and community can debate and refine the idea.
4. The dev reviews popular/well-discussed suggestions periodically.

## Tips for good suggestions

- **One idea per `/suggest`** - don't pack 10 features into one submission.
- **Explain why** - what problem does this solve, who benefits?
- **Be specific** - "add more variety" is too vague; "add a `/poker tournament` mode with brackets" is actionable.
- **Search the suggestions channel first** - if your idea has already been suggested, react/comment instead of duplicating.

## Cooldown

`/suggest` has a **5-minute cooldown** (300 seconds). Take your time - write a clear suggestion rather than spamming half-baked ones.

## Don't use /suggest for…

- **Bugs** - use [`/bugreport`](report-bugs.md) instead.
- **Personal payment issues** - open a Discord ticket.
- **Complaints** - use the appropriate support channel.
