# Upgrades

Paradise Bot has progression and upgrade systems across most major features. Here's where they all live:

## Bank vault upgrades

15 tiers from Basic ($1B capacity) to Multiverse ($1Q capacity). Prices run from $500M up to hundreds of trillions.

→ [`/shop`](../features/store/shop.md)

## Mining

- **14 pickaxe tiers** (Wooden → Transcendent), levels 1–200.
- Buyable with money through Diamond; **craft-only** above (Obsidian → Transcendent require ores + money).
- **Perks unlocked at level milestones** - +5%/+10%/+15% ore value at lvl 5/25/45, etc.
- **5 active abilities** unlocked via perks.

→ [Mining index](../features/mining/index.md)

## Fishing

- **13 fishing rod tiers** (Basic → Transcendent), levels 1–500.
- All buyable with money (no crafting); rods can be sold back at 60%.
- **13 fishing locations** unlock at level milestones.

→ [Fishing index](../features/fishing/index.md)

## Pets

- **One pet, levels 1–200**, then prestige.
- Perks scale automatically with level: faster cooldowns, bigger payouts, less fatigue.
- Higher levels = exponentially bigger work earnings.

→ [Pets index](../features/pets/index.md)

## Farming

- **11 crop tiers** (Wheat → Diamond Melon).
- **3 fertilizer tiers** for grow-time boosts.
- **Auto-replant** unlocked for donators.
- **Patreon profit/speed boosts** stack on every crop.

→ [Farming index](../features/farming/index.md)

## Heists

- Daily limit scales with Patreon tier (4 → 15+) plus +2 bonus for voting.
- Heist tax decreases with tier (20% → 0% at Tier 6+).

→ [Heists index](../games/heists/index.md)

## Scratch cards

- **Weekly free claim** for donators (10 cards / 15 for Tier 8).
- Can win Patreon gifts, money, farm boosts, lottery tickets, jackpots.

→ [Scratch Tickets index](../economy/scratch/index.md)

## Restaurants

- **Fries shop** has its own level/XP/prestige system + buyable upgrades (Insulated Bag, GPS Navigation, Premium Customers, Weather Gear).

→ [Restaurants index](../features/restaurants/index.md)

## Patreon (real money)

The biggest upgrade - Patreon tiers unlock permanent buffs across **every system listed above** plus 0% fees and bigger limits everywhere.

→ [Patreon Shop](../features/store/patreon.md)
