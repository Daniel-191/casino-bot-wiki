# Rules

Paradise Bot is a fair-play environment. These rules cover what's not allowed and what gets you banned.

## Hard bans (immediate)

- **Exploit abuse** - duping money, abusing race conditions, or otherwise gaming bugs in the bot's economy.
- **Selfbots / automation** - running scripts that auto-spam commands, click buttons, or chain rewards.
- **Multi-accounting** - using multiple Discord accounts to farm voting rewards, daily, etc.
- **Real-money trading (RMT)** - selling in-game currency for real money is forbidden.
- **Harassment / abuse** in the Paradise support server.

## Soft warnings → temporary bans

- **Begging in non-beg channels** for free money from staff.
- **Spamming** the same command repeatedly to clog the bot.
- **Failing captcha** (`/verify`) repeatedly may lead to a temporary lock.

## Fair play

- **One account per person.** Alts will be banned (yours and the alt).
- **Bot uptime is best-effort.** No SLA is guaranteed.
- **Maintenance mode** can lock commands during updates - wait it out.

## Refunds

- **Patreon donations are non-refundable.** They support development; they don't entitle you to refunds for losing streaks.
- **In-game money lost in games is not refundable.** That's how gambling works.
- **Bug-related refunds** are case-by-case - open a support ticket.

## Anti-selfbot

The bot runs random captchas via `/verify`. Failing or skipping these can flag your account.

## Reporting violations

- Open a ticket in the support Discord (use [`/links`](../features/other/links.md)).
- Provide the offending user's Discord ID and screenshots if relevant.
- Staff reviews reports manually.
