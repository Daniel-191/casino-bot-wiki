# Supporting the Bot

Paradise Bot is built and hosted by one developer. There are several ways to keep the lights on.

## Voting (free, no money required)

Vote daily on the bot listing sites. **Voting on both within 24 hours gives you bonus rewards in-game** like extra heist runs and a free scratch card.

| Site | Link |
|---|---|
| **Top.gg** | [vote here](https://top.gg/bot/1272208314163396650/vote) |
| **Discord Bot List** | [vote here](https://discordbotlist.com/bots/paradise-7069/upvote) |

Run [`/vote`](../economy/earning/vote.md) for the in-game voting hub. Each vote drops a [scratch card](../economy/scratch/index.md) too.

## Patreon (real money, big in-game perks)

Real-money tier subscriptions at [patreon.com/ParadiseBotx](https://patreon.com/ParadiseBotx). 8 tiers from DONATOR ($) to GODLY ($$$). Perks include:

- **Massive farming boosts** - up to **3× profit, 3× faster grow time** (Tier 8).
- **0% heist tax** (Tier 6+).
- **+25% mining earnings** + Mine All button.
- **15 lottery tickets** vs. 10 free.
- **10–15 free scratch cards/week**.
- **Bigger heist daily limits** (up to 17 with voting).
- **Discord role flair**.

See the [Patreon Shop](../features/store/patreon.md) page for the full tier-by-tier breakdown.

## PayPal donations (one-time)

For one-off support without the Patreon commitment:

[PayPal donation form](https://www.paypal.com/donate/?hosted_button_id=W7KDRFDEETEFU)

Donations don't unlock perks but do help cover hosting costs.

## Word of mouth

Sharing the bot with friends, posting about it, recommending it on r/discordbots - all genuinely helps growth.

## Bug reports & feedback

Free, but valuable: open issues on [Github](https://github.com/Daniel-191/Paradise) or use the support Discord. See the [Support](../support/index.md) section.
