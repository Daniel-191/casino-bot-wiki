# About Paradise Bot

Paradise Bot is a Discord-first **simulated gambling and economy bot** with deep progression systems, multiplayer games, and an active development roadmap.

## What it does

Paradise Bot ties together a wide range of mechanics in one place:

- **16 casino games** - blackjack, poker, slots, roulette, baccarat, dice, crash, tower, minesweeper, plinko, wheel-of-fortune, horse-race, sportsbet, uncrossable, RPS, coinflip.
- **14 multiplayer games** - battleship, connect4, tic-tac-toe, wordle, fight, coinflip duel, dice duel, russian roulette, buckshot, murder mystery, RPS multiplayer, multiplayer dice, multidice, multiplayer blackjack.
- **Progression systems** - pets (level 1–200), mining (14 pickaxes / 13 depths), fishing (13 rods / 13 locations), farming (11 crops with real-time growth).
- **Heists** - co-op multiplayer + solo, with 8 locations and a daily Weekly Major Heist.
- **Restaurants** - Fries delivery minigame.
- **Lottery** - daily community drawing fueled by ticket sales and game taxes.
- **Banking** - wallet vs bank split, daily interest, 15 vault tiers.
- **Scratch cards** from voting + Patreon perks across the entire bot.

## Built by

Mal023 (one developer). Active development since 2024+. Hosted in the Paradise community Discord with a small staff team for moderation and Patreon management.

## Tech

- **py-cord** - Discord bot library.
- **SQLite** - sharded databases for performance (e.g., farming has 10 shards).
- **Python 3.14** runtime.
- **MkDocs Material** for this wiki.
