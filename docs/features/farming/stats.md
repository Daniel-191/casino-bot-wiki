# /farming-stats

Detailed lifetime farming statistics - totals planted, harvested, money earned, favorite crop, and performance metrics.

## Description

The numbers-on-a-page view of your farming history. Shows everything from how many crops you've ever planted to your total fertilizer spend.

## Usage

`/farming-stats`

## Examples

`/farming-stats`

## What it shows

The stats embed surfaces lifetime numbers across all your slots and shards:

- **Crops Planted (lifetime)** - total seeds you've put in the ground.
- **Crops Harvested** - total successful harvests.
- **Total Earned** - gross sell-price money from all harvests.
- **Total Spent on Seeds** - what you've paid out for seed costs.
- **Total Spent on Fertilizer** - fertilizer outlay.
- **Net Profit** - earned minus seed and fertilizer spend.
- **Favorite Crop** - most-planted crop type.
- **Most Used Fertilizer** - basic, super, mega, or none.
- **Patreon perks active** (if any) - current profit/speed boost from your tier.

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 10 seconds.
- Public - covers your own stats only.
- Auto-replant cycles count as normal harvests for stat purposes.
- For per-event impact, see [`/farming-events`](events.md). For the full crop catalog, see the [Farming index](index.md).
