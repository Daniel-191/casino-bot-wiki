# Patreon Shop

Real-money tier subscriptions to Paradise Bot via [Patreon](https://patreon.com/ParadiseBotx). 8 tiers, each unlocking permanent perks across the entire bot.

## Perks by system

### Farming

| Tier | Profit boost | Speed boost |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | +15% | +10% |
| Tier 2 | +25% | +15% |
| Tier 3 | +30% | +20% |
| Tier 4 | +50% | +25% |
| Tier 5 | +65% | +30% |
| Tier 6 | +80% | +35% |
| Tier 7 | +100% (2×) | +40% |
| Tier 8 | **+200% (3×)** | **3× faster (33% grow time)** |

Donators (T1+) also unlock **auto-replant** in farm settings.

### Heists

- **Tax rate** on winnings:
  - T1–T3: 15% (vs. 20% free)
  - T4–T5: 5%
  - T6–T8: **0%**
- **Daily heist limit:**
  - T1–T3: 6 (vs. 4 free)
  - T4–T5: 8
  - T6–T7: 10
  - T8: 15

### Mining

- **Mine All button** unlocked (instantly mine the whole grid page).
- **+25% bonus earnings** on every ore.

### Lottery

- **15 tickets per draw** (vs. 10 for free users).

### Scratch Cards

- **Weekly free claim:** 10 scratch cards every week (T1–T7) / **15** (T8).
- Better odds on legendary patreon-gift drops.

### Other

- **Discord role flair** + custom colored name in supported servers.
- Eligible for **Patreon gifts** via [`/scratch open`](../../economy/scratch/open.md) drops.
- Recipient of **stored Patreon gifts** for events and admin gifting.

## How to subscribe

Run [`/store`](store.md) for the link, or visit [patreon.com/ParadiseBotx](https://patreon.com/ParadiseBotx) directly.

## Checking your status

Use [`/patreon`](#) to see your current tier, status, and (for gifted memberships) the time remaining before expiry.

## Notes

- Active Patreon = your Patreon-linked Discord account is in the main Paradise server.
- Gifted Patreon (won via scratch cards or admin gift) is **time-limited** (typically 7 days for scratch wins).
- For stored gifts (admin-issued, redeemable later), use `/stored-patreon`.
- See [`/scratch open`](../../economy/scratch/index.md) for the rare-but-legitimate way to win free Patreon time.
