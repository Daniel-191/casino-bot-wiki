# Banking System

Store your money safely and earn daily interest.

## How Banking Works

Your bank account is separate from your wallet and keeps your money safe from robberies. You earn interest daily on money in your bank.

### Safety Features
- **Protected from robbing** - Money in your bank cannot be stolen by other players
- **Daily interest** - Earn money automatically every day at 11:00 UTC

## Interest Rates

Interest is calculated based on your bank balance:

- **Up to $5B:** 10% daily interest
- **$5B - $50B:** 9% daily interest  
- **$50B - $500B:** 8% daily interest
- **$500B - $2T:** 7% daily interest
- **$2T - $10T:** 6% daily interest
- **$10T - $50T:** 5% daily interest
- **$50T+:** 4% daily interest

**Maximum daily interest:** $15 Trillion per day

## Bank Tax

Large bank accounts may be taxed periodically:
- **Tax rate:** 5% of bank balance
- **Purpose:** Funds the community lottery system

Inactive users (2+ days without activity) face a 2% tax on wallet balance.

## Commands

### Basic Banking
- `/deposit <amount>` - Put money in your bank (minimum $100)
- `/withdraw <amount>` - Take money out of your bank (minimum $100)
- `/balance` - Check your wallet and bank balance

### Special Keywords
- `max` - Deposit/withdraw maximum possible amount
- `half` - Deposit/withdraw half of available money
- `k/m/b` suffixes supported (e.g., "100m" = $100,000,000)

### Other Commands
- `/shop` - Buy bank vault upgrades
- `/transactions` - View your banking history

## Tips

1. **Keep money in bank** - Earn interest and stay safe from robberies
2. **Upgrade your vault** - Bigger vaults let you store more money
3. **Check daily** - Interest is paid at 11:00 UTC every day
4. **Plan ahead** - If your bank is full, interest goes to your wallet instead
