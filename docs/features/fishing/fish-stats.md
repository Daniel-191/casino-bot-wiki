# /fish-stats

Detailed lifetime fishing statistics - catches, earnings, level, biggest catch, location breakdown.

## Description

The numbers-on-a-page view of your fishing career. Shows everything tracked across every cast you've ever made.

## Usage

`/fish-stats`

## Examples

`/fish-stats`

## What it shows

- **Level + XP** - current level and progress toward the next.
- **Equipped rod** - your active rod with all its bonuses.
- **Total Catches** - lifetime fish caught.
- **Total Earnings** - lifetime money from selling fish.
- **Current streak / Longest streak** - consecutive successful casts (no events disrupt streaks).
- **Last catch time** - relative timestamp of your most recent catch.
- **Catches by location** - count per location (helps you spot which spots you're under-using).
- **Catches by rarity** - how many Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical you've pulled.

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 10 seconds.
- Read-only - no actions from this view; use [`/fish`](fish.md) to cast or manage rods.
- Stats update immediately after each cast.
- For the full rod/location ladder, see the [Fishing index](index.md).
- Equal-payout alternative system: [Pets](../pets/index.md).
