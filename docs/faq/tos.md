# What is the Terms of Service?

**Short answer:** A required agreement covering acceptable use, ban policies, and refund/dispute rules. Run [`/tos`](../features/other/tos.md) and click Accept once to use the bot.

## Why it exists

Paradise Bot is a **simulated gambling** bot. The TOS clarifies:

- **No real money is at stake.** All "money" is fictitious in-game currency.
- **Patreon donations are not refundable.** They support the bot's development; they don't entitle you to specific game outcomes.
- **Bans for cheating, exploit abuse, or harassment** are at the developer's discretion.
- **The bot is provided as-is** - no warranty, no guarantee of uptime.
- **Privacy** - what data the bot stores (user IDs, balances, transaction logs) and how long.

## Accepting

1. Run `/tos`.
2. Click the green **Accept** button (60-second timeout).
3. Done - your acceptance is stored. You can use any command going forward.

## Re-reading later

Run `/tos` again - if you've already accepted, the bot shows the links so you can re-read.

## Disputes / appeals

If you've been banned and believe it's a mistake:

1. Open a ticket in the support Discord (use [`/links`](../features/other/links.md)).
2. Provide your Discord ID and a brief explanation.
3. The bot owner reviews appeals manually.
