# /scratch weekly-scratch

Donators-only free weekly scratch card claim. Claim once per week to add 10 (or 15) cards to your account.

## Description

Active donators (any Patreon tier) can claim a weekly batch of free scratch cards. The cards drop straight into your account - open them with `/scratch open` whenever you want.

This is one of the perks of supporting Paradise Bot on Patreon.

## Usage

`/scratch weekly-scratch`

## Examples

`/scratch weekly-scratch`

## Cards per claim

| Donator tier | Cards per week |
|---|---|
| **Tier 1 – 7** (DONATOR through CRAZY) | **10** |
| **Tier 8** (GODLY) | **15** |
| Non-donator | ❌ Locked |

## Reset timing

- The week resets on a fixed schedule - the embed shows the **next reset timestamp** when you've already claimed.
- After reset, the claim is available again.

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 5 seconds (slash-command cooldown - the weekly limit is enforced separately).
- **Donators only.** Non-donators see a Patreon prompt instead of a claim.
- Cards stack with anything you already have - they don't replace, they add.
- See the [Scratch Tickets index](index.md) for what cards can win.
- For the donator membership itself, see the [Patreon shop](../../features/store/patreon.md).
