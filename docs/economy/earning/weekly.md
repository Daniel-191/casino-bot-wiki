# /weekly

The weekly money reward - bigger than `/daily`, with the same multiplier system.

## Description

Pays out $375,000,000 base, multiplied by your **vote streak** and **Patreon tier**. Available once every 7 days.

A typical free user with a 30-day vote streak earns $937.5M per claim. A Godly patron with a Legendary streak earns up to $15B.

## Usage

`/weekly`

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 7 days.
- **Base reward:** $375M.
- **Multipliers:** vote streak (up to 10×) × Patreon (up to 40×).
- Patreon does **not** reduce this command's cooldown.
- Pair with [`/monthly`](monthly.md) and [`/daily`](daily.md) for the full reward stack.
