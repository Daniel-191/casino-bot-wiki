# /beg

Beg the streets for a small handout.

## Description

Pays a random amount between **$10,000 and $100,000** every 15 seconds. The smallest income source in the bot, but it has the shortest cooldown - useful when you've burned through your wallet and need anything to keep playing.

## Usage

`/beg`

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 15 seconds.
- **Reward:** random $10K – $100K (uniform distribution).
- Patreon supporters get a **35% cooldown reduction** on this command (~10s instead of 15s).
- No multipliers - vote streak and Patreon tier do **not** boost the amount.
- For bigger income at longer cooldowns, see [`/hourly`](hourly.md), [`/daily`](daily.md), [`/crime`](../crime/crime.md).
