# /hourly

Hourly paycheck with a streak bonus that grows the longer you keep collecting on time.

## Description

Pays $7,500,000 base, plus $250,000 per hour of streak (up to 24 hours of streak), all multiplied by your vote streak. Maintain the hourly streak by claiming within 2 hours of the previous claim - miss the window and your streak resets to 1.

At max streak (24 hours), the streak bonus alone is $6M, bringing the unmultiplied reward to $13.5M per hour.

## Usage

`/hourly`

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 1 hour.
- **Base reward:** $7.5M.
- **Streak bonus:** +$250K per streak hour, capped at 24.
- **Grace period:** 2 hours. Claim within 2h of last claim to keep streak; miss it and streak resets to 1.
- Vote streak multiplier applies to the total (base + streak bonus).
- Patreon does **not** reduce this command's cooldown.
- See [`/hourly-stats`] for full streak history (separate command).
