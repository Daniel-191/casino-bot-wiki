# /daily

Claim your once-a-day money reward.

## Description

Pays out $75,000,000 base, multiplied by your **vote streak** and **Patreon tier** if you have either. Available once every 24 hours. The response shows the full reward breakdown so you can see exactly where the multipliers came from.

## Usage

`/daily`

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 24 hours.
- **Base reward:** $75M.
- **Multipliers:** vote streak (1.25× to 10×) and Patreon (up to 40×). They stack.
- The cooldown is **not** reduced by Patreon for this command.
- Boost your reward by maintaining a vote streak - see [`/vote`](vote.md).
- Use [`/cooldowns`](../money-management/cooldowns.md) to check when your next claim is available.
