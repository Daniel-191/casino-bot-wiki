# /monthly

The biggest single recurring payout - a once-per-month windfall.

## Description

Pays out $2,000,000,000 base, multiplied by your **vote streak** and **Patreon tier**. Available once every 30 days.

This is the largest recurring reward in the bot. With max multipliers (40× Patreon × 10× streak) it pays $800B per claim.

## Usage

`/monthly`

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 30 days.
- **Base reward:** $2B.
- **Multipliers:** vote streak (up to 10×) × Patreon (up to 40×). Stacks for up to 400×.
- Patreon does **not** reduce this command's cooldown - set a calendar reminder.
- Don't forget to claim before reset on day 30 - the cooldown is wall-clock, not from-last-claim.
