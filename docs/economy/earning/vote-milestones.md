# /vote-milestones

View the permanent unlocks you get for maintaining a vote streak.

## Description

Shows your current vote streak alongside three milestone unlocks. Each milestone is **active only while your streak is at or above the threshold** - drop below and you lose the perk (and in the case of property slots, may have properties auto-sold).

## Milestones

| Streak | Unlock | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **10 days** | Property Protection | 70% fewer random property events (taxes, repairs, etc.) |
| **15 days** | Extra Property Slots | +5 property slots (15 total instead of 10) |
| **30 days** | Extra Farming Slot | +1 permanent farming slot |

## Usage

`/vote-milestones`

## Notes

- **Cooldown:** 3 seconds.
- Milestones are **active**, not earned-once. Drop below the threshold and the bonus turns off.
- **Property slot warning:** if your streak drops below 15 and you currently own more than 10 properties, the excess will be **auto-sold at 70% refund value**. Don't let your streak break if you're using the extra slots.
- **Farming slot:** the 30-day farming slot is added/removed automatically as your streak crosses 30.
- Streak is maintained by voting at least once every 12 hours on Top.gg or DBL - see [`/vote`](vote.md).
